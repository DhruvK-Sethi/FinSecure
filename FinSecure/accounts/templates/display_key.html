Understanding Our Zero-Trust Model

At FinSecure, we prioritize your privacy and data security above all else. Our zero-trust model ensures that your sensitive financial information remains protected, even from us.

  • Your data is encrypted using AES-256 bit encryption, the gold standard in data security.
  • The encryption key is generated based on your password and is never stored on our servers.
  • Without this key, your data cannot be decrypted or accessed by anyone, including our staff.
  • You have full control over your data's accessibility.

Remember: If you lose your password, we cannot recover your data. Please store it securely!

How It Works

  1. You set a strong, unique encryption password.
  2. We use this password to generate a secure encryption key.
  3. Your data is encrypted using this key before storage.
  4. The key is then discarded and never stored on our servers.
  5. To access your data, you'll need to provide your password each time.
